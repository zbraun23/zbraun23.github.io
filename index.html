Skiing Hakuba

Eli decided to leave Hakuba early because he had had enough of the snow. I was determined to stay even though the conditions were poor, so Zac and I continued to hit the slopes. Our last 2 days in Hakuba were a blizzard.

We went over to the back side of the mountain which was much less touched and actually found some amazing powder. One of the days we went into a nice, new cafe place and had soup. It was freezing but we still had an awesome time together on the mountain and then onsenning at the ryokan.

Our First Night
